Who Are We?

At Seller Integrity, we are more than consultants. We are experienced Amazon marketplace specialists dedicated to helping sellers navigate some of the platform's most complex challenges.

Built on firsthand experience within Amazon's Seller Partner Support (SPS) ecosystem, we understand how Amazon evaluates account performance, compliance concerns, operational risks, and seller behaviour. This perspective allows us to analyze issues beyond surface-level symptoms and focus on the underlying factors that influence Amazon's decision making process.

Our approach combines operational excellence, structured root-cause analysis, and data-driven precision. Our ultimate mission is to bridge the gap between sellers and Amazon's strict compliance matrices, bringing transparency, trust, and proven strategies to protect and scale your global e-commerce business.

FAQs

Q: Can you guarantee account reinstatement? +

A: No. Amazon makes all final decisions regarding account status. Our role is to help sellers understand Amazon's concerns, identify root causes, and develop the strongest possible response strategy.

Q: How quickly can you review my case? +

A: Most cases can be reviewed within 24-48 hours after receiving the required information and documentation.

Q: What information do you need to get started? +

A: We typically require Amazon's notification, account details, relevant documentation, and a brief overview of the issue to conduct an initial assessment.

Q: Can you help if my appeal has already been rejected? +

A: Yes. Many sellers contact us after previous appeals have been unsuccessful. We review prior submissions, identify gaps, and help develop a stronger strategy.

Q: Do you charge anything upfront to review my case? +

A: No. We offer a complimentary initial case evaluation to understand your situation, review the available information, and determine whether we can assist. This allows you to gain clarity on your options before making any commitment.
